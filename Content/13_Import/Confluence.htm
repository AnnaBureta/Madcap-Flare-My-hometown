When I googled "Confluence documentation" the search took me to Confluence Documentation Directory where one can find the documentation for all versions of the software, including the latest one:

For a logged-in user, navigating to the Confluence documentation should not be a problem either:

Confluence - Pros

  1. Rather easy to find
  2. You can click on and enlarge the images in the documentation, e.g. here
  3. Informative yet funny tutorials in the Get Started section of the documentation: Navigate Confluence and Space Ace - I loved the aeronautics references
  4. Informal language, e.g. Enough about that; let's begin. here

Confluence - Cons

  1. The Next button is all blue unless you hover your mouse over it:
  2. On some pages, you are unable to enlarge the images, e.g. here
  3. Some articles are a little bit too wordy, I would definitely try to make them shorter, e.g. here
